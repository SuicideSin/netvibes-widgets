UWA Widgets

  • Netvibes Customize @ ecosystem

    You can change some appearance preferences like background, opacity.

  • Quick Translator @ ecosystem

    Just write text in one box and read translated text in other, quick and easy :) You can use all avaiable languages from Google Language Tool.

  • Last.fm Statistics @ ecosystem

    Displays last.fm (audioscrobbler) statistics, with autorefresh function.

  • myPlayer @ ecosystem

    Bring MySpace player to your page.

  • Google Suggest @ ecosystem

    Displays google search bar with suggestions.

Mini-api widgets

  • Gmail Quota @ ecosystem

    Displays current total size of GMAIL.

  • Last.fm Radio Player @ ecosystem

    Early version of last.fm player. You able to play your neiborhood station and watch what currently playing. You can’t send any commands like skip, ban, love - the don’t work as I expected.

  • Metal Express Radio Tune @ ecosystem

    Displays current Metal Radio Express tune.

  • Search & Suggest @ ecosystem

    Search & Sugest is a search box that can search many engines, and display suggestion from google.
